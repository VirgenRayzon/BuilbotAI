# PSU Tier List (2024-2025 Edition)

This guide ranks Power Supply Units (PSUs) based on component quality, voltage regulation, and safety protections. A high-quality PSU is the most critical component for system longevity.

## Tier A: High-End (Gold/Platinum/Titanium)
*Highly recommended for high-end gaming (RTX 4080/4090) and workstation builds.*

*   **Corsair:** RMx (2021/2024), RMe, HXi, AX Series.
*   **Seasonic:** Focus GX/PX, Prime GX/PX/TX, Vertex Series (ATX 3.0).
*   **EVGA:** SuperNOVA G6, G7, P6, T2.
*   **MSI:** MPG A-G Series, MEG Ai-P Series.
*   **Thermaltake:** Toughpower GF3 / TF1.
*   **be quiet!:** Dark Power 13, Straight Power 12.
*   **Asus:** ROG Thor, ROG Strix Aura Edition.

## Tier B: Mid-Range (Gold/Bronze)
*Great for mid-range builds (RTX 4060/4070, RX 7700 XT).*

*   **Corsair:** CX-F RGB, TX-M Series.
*   **Cooler Master:** MWE Gold V2 (Non-Full Range is Tier B/C).
*   **EVGA:** SuperNOVA G5, GD, GQ.
*   **Seasonic:** Core GC/GM/GX.
*   **Deepcool:** DQ-M-V2L.

## Tier C: Budget (Bronze)
*Acceptable for entry-level gaming or office PCs.*

*   **Corsair:** CV Series (Avoid 450W if possible), CX Series (2023).
*   **EVGA:** BR, BQ Series.
*   **Cooler Master:** MWE Bronze V2.
*   **Thermaltake:** Smart BM2.

## Tier E: AVOID / Dangerous
*Units with known issues, poor component quality, or missing vital protections (OPP/OTP).*

*   **Gigabyte:** P750GM / P850GM (Early revisions known for explosive failure).
*   **Thermaltake:** Smart RGB / Smart 80+ White (Very poor component quality).
*   **Most "Generic" or "Korean/Chinese" no-brand PSUs.**
*   **Apevia:** Most models.

---
> [!IMPORTANT]  
> Always check for **ATX 3.0 / 3.1 compatibility** if using a modern NVIDIA card with the 12VHPWR connector to avoid using adapters.
